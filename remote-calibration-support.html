How Remote Calibration Support Typically Works.

The goal is to identify the real bottleneck quickly, reduce wasted time, and create a stable path forward.

1. Project Review: vehicle setup, ECU platform, engine package, hardware list, current issue, and project goals.
2. Data Collection: datalogs, calibration files, screenshots, scope captures, dyno information, and troubleshooting notes.
3. Analysis & Troubleshooting: review of calibration behavior, sensor data, startup behavior, boost control, CAN communication, and drivability.
4. Direction & Validation: structured next-step guidance, calibration refinement, validation strategy, and follow-up support.
