Common EcuTek Calibration Problems We Help Solve.

Many drivability and boost-control issues are caused by torque strategy, airflow modeling, compensations, or hardware interaction.

Boost-control instability: throttle closure, boost oscillation, wastegate behavior, and torque intervention.
Fueling correction issues: MAF scaling, SD behavior, ethanol blending, transient fueling, and AFR stability.
Drivability problems: surge, hesitation, tip-in response, cruise instability, and throttle sensitivity.
Knock behavior: false knock review, ignition correction, thermal sensitivity, and timing strategy.
Flex fuel setup: sensor scaling, ethanol compensation, startup behavior, and blending strategy.
OEM torque strategy: throttle interaction, load limits, boost targeting, and ECU intervention behavior.
