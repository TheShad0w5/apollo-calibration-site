Common Emtron Problems We Help Diagnose.

Most calibration issues are not solved by changing one table. They require understanding how the ECU, sensors, wiring, engine package, and strategy interact.

Trigger and sync issues: crank/cam setup, edge selection, missing-tooth patterns, scope review, sensor polarity, and RPM instability.
Startup problems: cranking fuel, ignition timing validation, injector timing, trigger offset, base timing lock, and cold-start behavior.
Fueling errors: injector characterization, pressure reference, dead time, fuel density, ethanol content strategy, and transient response.
DBW and idle control: throttle behavior, idle airflow, target control, startup flare, decay behavior, and stability problems.
Boost control instability: wastegate behavior, dome control, closed-loop setup, compensations, and protection strategies.
CAN and dash integration: device communication, termination, baud rate, channel mapping, warning logic, and validation.
